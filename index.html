About B&M

Locally owned. Professionally run.

B&M Signs and Advertising is a locally owned portable sign rental company based right here in Lloydminster. We serve businesses, events, and organizations on both the Alberta and Saskatchewan sides of the border, plus surrounding towns.

Every sign in our fleet is a double sided, powder coated steel frame 4×8 unit with weighted bases for prairie wind, interchangeable letter tracks, and full panel vinyl options. We handle the permit filing with the City's Development Services department, the site assessment, the delivery, and the message changes, so you just tell us what to say.

What we do

Everything from permit to pickup.

Turnkey service. The only thing you have to do is tell us the message. We handle the rest.

Permit Handling

We file the temporary sign permit with the City of Lloydminster on your behalf and track it through to approval. Setback compliant placement, every time.

Design & Graphics

Letter track messages included with every rental. Full colour vinyl panel design and production coordinated with our local print partner when you want the premium look.

Delivery & Install

We transport, position, and ballast every sign on site, levelled, sandbagged against prairie wind, and positioned for maximum sightline from the nearest roadway.

Message Changes

On site letter swaps or vinyl panel replacements as you run new promotions. Annual clients get free changes built into the contract.

Questions & answers

Good to know.

The things most new clients want to understand before picking up the phone.

How much does it cost?
Pricing depends on your campaign length, location, and whether you need custom vinyl graphics or just letter track messages. We keep it simple and affordable for local Lloyd businesses. Send us a quick message and we'll get back to you the same day with a tailored quote.
Do I need a City permit?
Yes. Every portable sign placed within Lloydminster city limits requires a temporary sign permit from Development Services. We file it for you and manage the renewals.
What size are the signs?
4 feet high by 8 feet wide per side, double sided, so your message is visible from both directions of traffic. Powder coated steel frame, weighted base, levelling feet, and ground clearance for winter snow.
Where can the sign be placed?
On your commercial property, behind the required setbacks: 1.5 m from the property line, 3 m from driveways or access points, and 5 m from corner intersections. We handle the site assessment, so you don't need to measure anything.
Can I change the message mid rental?
Yes. Monthly clients can request on site changes any time, and annual clients get free message changes built into the contract. Reach out for the current change schedule.
How fast can you install?
Standard turnaround is about 7 to 12 business days once you sign, factoring in the City's 5 to 10 day permit review. Rush options are available when the permit and sign are ready.
What areas do you serve?
Lloydminster (both Alberta and Saskatchewan sides) plus surrounding towns. That includes Marshall, Lashburn, Kitscoty, Marwayne, and parts of the RM of Wilton.
What if there's a windstorm?
Every sign is ballasted with sandbags on a weighted steel base. When Environment Canada issues wind warnings above 80 km/h we proactively inspect and, if needed, temporarily lay signs down. You're never liable for weather damage to our equipment.
Do you design the graphics for me?
Absolutely. Basic letter track messages (standard black letters on white) are included. For a professional custom look we design full colour vinyl panels, coordinated with our local print vendor and built for readability at prairie highway speeds.
Can I supply my own artwork?
Yes. If you have existing brand files we'll lay them out for 4×8 vinyl and send a proof before printing. Vector PDFs or high resolution PNGs work best.
Do you work with nonprofits?
Yes. We offer discounted rates for registered nonprofits, churches, sports leagues, and community organizations running fundraisers or events. Just mention it when you reach out.
What if the sign gets vandalized?
We cover graffiti cleanup and incidental wear at no cost. Our rental agreement only holds you responsible for damage caused by negligence or by a third party you're responsible for on your property, which is a rare occurrence.
